su real

close

“When he’s not playing mad professor in his West Delhi beat lab, he’s as likely to be tearing up a festival green room or inciting a thousand simultaneous heart attacks from behind the decks.” – GQ

“While fellow producer/DJ Nucleya has spearheaded the genre of desi bass, it is Su Real who has added weight to the genre and converted it into a movement.” – Mumbai Mirror

Popular culture in India is evolving rapidly, and Su Real is at the forefront of that movement. The old divisions – Bollywood, commercial, trance – no longer make sense. Su Real represents this new breed of Indian music producers who create and curate for the modern Indian dancefloor.

In this new era of viral trends and overnight sensations, Su Real has evolved into a known constant in India’s musical underground. Returning home to Delhi from 10 years as PR Specialist in New York and Montreal to a growing local scene in 2008, he dove right in - programming the events and cultural activities at the legendary T.L.R., the first venue that lit up Hauz Khas Village with non-commercial music and alternative arts. He first gained notoriety as a DJ in 2010 with his Disco 31 nights at T.L.R. There, he developed his sound & crowd until in 2013, Rolling Stone India declared “Su Real is the legendary secret ingredient to some of Delhi’s recent favourite underground sets/parties/combinations thereof.”

Taking his trademark style across the country, Su Real shared the stage with a veritable who’s who of dance music today: Major Lazer, Skrillex, Diplo, Jason Derulo, Yellow Claw, NGHTMRE, JSTJR, Pretty Lights, Hudson Mohawke, MC Zulu, Alo Wala, among several others. Nucleya, the reigning king of Indian EDM has long been a benefactor, offering him key support slots on his tours & album launches. Su Real has also performed at major music festivals including Sunburn, Vh1 Supersonic, NH7 Weekender, Timeout72, MTV Bloc Party, India Bike Week and more.

Vidyut is a fusion post-prog band based out of Chennai. Since the time Vidyut was formed back in 2017, it has got various achievements. Having won prizes in various band competitions in colleges like SRM, MCC, Veltech, IFMR, etc.and also Forum Rock On, the band has always made an impact on the crowd. Their music is something very different from most of the bands out there. Influenced by band and musicians like Agam, Thaikuddam Bridge, Fossils, Mohiner Ghoraguli, Dream Theater, Alter Bridge, Opeth, Porcupine tree, Periphery, Plini, Intervals, etc., Vidyut sounds a lot like them yet somehow they sound completely different. The band has also played in various shows outside of competitions. Some of them are The Unwind Center, Chennai., The Forum Vijaya Mall, Chennai.(Rock On winners), Indian Youth Conclave by The Climber, Chennai., Lake View Resort, Tajpur, West Bengal, Oyezter Bay, Mandarmani, West Bengal, etc.

The band members comprise of Saptarshi Chakraborty (Vocals), Ayan Choudhury (guitar), Snehashish Das (bass/backing vocals), Melvin Linus (drums).
