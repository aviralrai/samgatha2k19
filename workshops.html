Travel Photography

Srivatsan S is Travel photo blogger by passion. A self taught photographer, he mastered the nuances of photography through dedication and an unanswering attitude towards learning in life. His pictures evidently speak of the very relationship between Mother Nature and the people that nurture her. He believes that the best way to explore and experience a certain location is through blending into the indigenous culture, heritage and lifestyles.

He has numerous accolades accredited to his name. He has won "Shoot the frame award" in 2012 and was also one of the "Top 10 Tamron winners" in 2013. His work won the Travel photographer of the year from India Photo Award in 2017. His travelogues have been published in more than 20 magazines including National Geographic Traveler. Apart from this, he is also the founder of Madras photo bloggers, a co-founder Beaumoments, co-founder of Colors and trails.
